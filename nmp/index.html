New Maps Plus Portfolio

These projects were created for the University of Kentucky's New Maps Plus
Graduate Certificate in Digital Mapping Program

Hometown Map

Although I've called many places home, I selected Warm Springs, Virginia as the location for my hometown map assignment. Tooltips provide detail for points of interest.

Cancer Death Rates

Hosted on CARTO, this map shows cancer death rates based on age-adjusted data.

Alternative Energy

The distribution of nuclear and solar power plant locations is shown, with capacity visualized using proportional symbols. Data is loaded using an external geoJSON file.

Rental Prices

Median Rental prices in the United States are shown on this chloropleth map. By using jQuery, the data is loaded onto the map with an AJAX request.

Amenities in Denver, CO

Fire Stations, After School Programs and Food Stores are shown on this map. Interactive functions allow the user to filter sites within 1 km and turn layers on and off.

National Park Visits

This proportional symbol map shows average annual visitation rates on National Park Service Units. Data was gathered from the NPS Integrated Resource Management Application
